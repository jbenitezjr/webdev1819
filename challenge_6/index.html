Step 1

Separate the slicer from the packaging.

Step 2

Gather and peel a banana.

Step 3

Place banana on a flat surface.

Step 4

Place slicer on banana so that it covers the entirety.

Step 5

Push down to slice.

Step 6

Enjoy a freshly sliced banana.
